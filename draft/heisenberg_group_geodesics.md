# Geodesics of Discrete Heisenberg Group

In works [1][2] the description of finite and infinite geodesics of a discrete Heisenberg group

```math
\mathbb{H}(\mathbb{Z}) \cong ⟨a, b| [[a,b], a] = 1, [[a, b], b] = 1⟩
```

is given. We would like to follow on the related open questions connected to that
study.

## References

[1](refs/1905.03226v1.pdf) Alexeev, Magdiev. The Language of Geodesics for the Discrete Heisenberg
Group
[2](refs/znsl6495.pdf) Vershik A. M., Malyutin A. V. Infinite geodesics in the discrete Heisenberg group.
